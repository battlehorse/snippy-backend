About Snippy

Snippy is a Chrome Extension that allows you to save important snippets you find online. Be it a new laptop or book you just spotted on Amazon, a set of interesting Tweets, a Wikipedia excerpt or even some citations from PubMed, use Snippy to collect them all.

Just go to a webpage of your choice and activate Snippy clicking the icon in the toolbar and then the Snip it! button. Then move your mouse over items you find interesting and click them. They will be immediately stored in your snippets!

Click the toolbar icon again and now choose Show snippets to review what you have collected so far. Snippets are automatically saved locally in your browser, so you won't lose them when you close Chrome.

If you want, you can also save your snippets online (via SnipBin) and share them with the world.
